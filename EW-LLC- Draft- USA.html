Payment
  • For all listings we accept payment by PayPal or credit/debit card via ebay.
  • All payments accepted in USD. The sales tax will be calculated at the checkout and is payable by the buyer and is not included in the product price.
  • Shipping only starts after receiving clear payments.
Shipping Policy

All orders are dispatched same or next business day. Free shipping if stated only applies to USA. We are only shipping within 48 continental United States.

We would be grateful if, when entering your DSRs for feedback, you would bear in mind that dispatch time is how quickly we ship your item and delivery time is how long the courier takes to get your item to you. Whilst we do everything to dispatch your item on time we have no control over the couriers' delivery times and must place our trust in their promise to deliver.

If you have an issue with the delivery time of one of our couriers, please e-mail us. We will always try to handle your queries as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Delivery area: We are only shipping within 48 continental United States.

Delivery method: USPS Priority & USPS First Class. Choose Priority for fast delivery.

Handling time: 1 business day after receiving payment. If USPS is open, we are open too.

Returns

We are dedicated towards aftersales care and if you have any problems with the product on delivery a full refund will be provided. Faulty items must be reported to us within 2 working days. Faulty products can be returned directly to us in original original packaging and we will send a replacement.

When no replacement is available a full refund will be issued. You should return to us with the order number and a description of the fault. All returns must be pre-authorized. Contact us immediately with the reason for your return.

We offer a 14 day 'No Quibble' returns policy. If you don't like the item you are responsible for return postage costs. The postage costs to send you the item will also be deducted. All goods must be unused, undamaged, in original packaging and in a re-salable condition and returned within the 14 days period from receipt.

Exchanges or a refund can be issued however unfortunately we cannot refund initial postage costs.

We strive to provide best possible service to our customers and we believe purchase from us is the beginning of long term relationship.

Note: Returns are at the Buyers Expense. 15% restocking fee will be charged on non defective items. Shipping and handling fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.

Your feedback is very important to us it affects our search placement on eBay. We will leave a positive feedback for you in return automatically once we receive your feedback.
